Types of Mice

Optical Mouse

Uses an LED and sensor to track movement on most surfaces.

Laser Mouse

Higher sensitivity using laser; works on more surfaces and higher DPI.

Mechanical (Ball)

Older design with a rolling ball — mostly obsolete now.

Mouse Anatomy

Left & Right Buttons

Primary controls for clicking and selecting.

Scroll Wheel

For vertical navigation; many wheels can also tilt or be clicked.

Sensor & DPI

DPI = dots per inch, higher = more cursor movement per physical inch.

Pro Tips

  • Adjust DPI for your workflow: lower for precision, higher for speed.
  • Use a mousepad optimized for your sensor type.
  • Keep firmware and drivers updated for additional features.
