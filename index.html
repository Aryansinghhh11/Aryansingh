Mouse History & Notes

The computer mouse was invented in the 1960s. Modern mice use optical or laser sensors for precise tracking. Gamers often choose high-DPI mice with programmable buttons, while designers may prefer low-latency wired mice.

Maintenance

  1. Keep the sensor area clean.
  2. Use a good mouse pad for consistent tracking.
  3. Replace batteries on wireless mice when needed.
